

## CodeAlpha Data Analytics Internship - Task 3


# 📖 Project Overview

This project focuses on transforming raw Brazilian E-commerce data into meaningful visualizations using Python.

The dashboard helps understand customer behavior, payment preferences, order trends, revenue distribution, product pricing, delivery performance, and customer satisfaction.

---

# 🎯 Objectives

- Convert raw data into meaningful visualizations
- Identify customer purchasing trends
- Analyze payment methods
- Understand customer reviews
- Discover monthly sales trends
- Analyze delivery performance
- Generate business insights

---

# 📂 Dataset

Brazilian Olist E-Commerce Dataset

Files Used:

- Customers
- Orders
- Order Items
- Payments
- Reviews
- Products

---

# 🛠 Technologies Used

# 📈 Key Business Insights

- São Paulo has the highest customer concentration.
- Credit cards are the most preferred payment method.
- Most customers give 5-star ratings.
- Orders are successfully delivered in most cases.
- Product prices are concentrated in the low-to-mid range.
- Monthly orders show seasonal variations.

# 💼 Business Recommendations

- Increase marketing in high-performing regions.
- Continue promoting credit card payments.
- Improve logistics for delayed deliveries.
- Expand customer acquisition in low-performing states.
- Use seasonal trends for inventory planning.
